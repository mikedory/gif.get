Animated gifs are amazing. They move, they inspire, and they are frequently completely absurd. They are spread across the internet in a bunch of amazing ways, but it's usually up to the user to keep track of the best ones known.

gif.get() aims to help with that. With one simple RESTful API, you can query for and filter by your favorite gifs, gif criteria, or gif-offering sites. And what's more, you can run your own copy anywhere you like!
